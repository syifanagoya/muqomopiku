Chauncey Billups Wife: Who Is Piper Billups?

Piper Billups is the adorable wife of head coach for the Portland Trail Blazers, Chauncey Billups.

Piper was born in Denver, Colorado, in the late 70s and attended Denver’s George Washington High School.

She bagged her first degree in Marketing from Metropolitan State College of Denver.

Piper and her husband Chauncey attended the same high school and  that was where they met, and they began to hang out.

She has been very supportive of his career and even when a sexual assault allegation was levelled against him in 1997, she stood by him.

The couple have three children together – Cydney, Ciara, and Cenaiya.

Their first daughter, Cydney, plays soccer and is a student of Sports Management at the University of Texas.

Who Is Chauncey Billups?

Chauncey Billups is an American basketball coach for the Portland Trail Blazers.

He had played in the NBA for 17 seasons, after her was drafted third overall by the Boston Celtics in the 1997 NBA draft.

All through his playing years he also played for the Toronto Raptors, Denver Nuggets, Minnesota Timberwolves, Detroit Pistons, New York Knicks, and Los Angeles Clippers during his NBA career.

While in college, he played basketball with the Colorado Buffaloes.

He won the NBA All-Star championship five times and earned the name “Mr. Big Shot” after he made late-game shots with Detroit.
