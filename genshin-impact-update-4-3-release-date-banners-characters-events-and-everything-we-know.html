Genshin Impact Update 4.3 Release Date, Banners, Characters, Events, And Everything We Know

Genshin Impact, the popular action role-playing game by miHoYo, is set to launch its latest update, 4.3, on December 20, 2023. This update is eagerly awaited by the game’s global fanbase, promising new characters, events, and exciting gameplay enhancements. This article delves into the specifics of Update 4.3, exploring its release schedule, new characters, weapon banners, and the various events and rewards that players can anticipate.

What Does the Update 4.3 Schedule Look Like?

The release of Genshin Impact 4.3 is scheduled following the game’s standard maintenance routine. It marks the end of Phase 2 of version 4.2, transitioning smoothly into the new update. Players worldwide can expect the update to be available on December 20, 2023, with time zone variations causing slight differences in release times. For example, players in the Eastern US can access the update on December 19 at 10 PM ET.

The preparation for this update is meticulous, with an option for players to pre-install starting December 18. This approach ensures a seamless experience for players eager to dive into the new content. The timing of the release across various regions like Central US, West US, Australia, Japan, Europe, and the UK varies, catering to a global audience.

What Are the New Features in Genshin Impact 4.3 Banners?

The Genshin Impact 4.3 update introduces exciting new characters and re-runs of fan favorites. One of the highlights is the introduction of Navia, a new 5-star character, and Chevreuse, a 4-star rate-up. Alongside these new faces, the update brings back popular characters such as Raiden Shogun, Ayaka, and Yoimiya. These characters are divided into two phases, each with its unique combination of characters and featured weapons. For instance, the first phase features Ayaka and Navia, while the second phase brings Yoimiya and Raiden Shogun into the spotlight.

Who Are the Genshin Impact 4.3 Characters?

Update 4.3 is not just about new faces; it’s about enriching the game’s universe with diverse characters. Navia, a five-star Geo DPS unit, and Ayaka, a Cryo DPS, are set to make their mark in the first phase. These characters bring unique abilities and backstory, enhancing the game’s narrative depth. The second half of the update focuses on Raiden Shogun and Yoimiya, offering players varied gameplay experiences. The update also teases the introduction of Chiori, a new character from Inazuma, expected to debut in version 4.4.

What Can Players Expect from Genshin Impact 4.3 Weapons?

The weapon banners in Genshin Impact 4.3 are as exciting as the character banners. The first phase highlights Navia’s signature weapon, “Verdict,” and Ayaka’s “Mistsplitter Reforged.” The second phase brings weapons like “Engulfing Lightning” and “Thundering Pulse” for Raiden and Yoimiya. These weapons are not just tools of combat; they are integral to the characters’ stories and playstyles, offering players new strategies and approaches in battles.

What Events and Awards Are Featured in Genshin Impact 4.3?

Genshin Impact 4.3 is packed with events that offer both entertainment and rewards. Players can participate in Fontaine’s Theatrics, Kuki’s Liyue Journey, and the Ultimate Overlord’s Mega Magic Sword event, among others. These events are not just about grinding for rewards; they are immersive experiences that expand the game’s world. Players can earn Primogems, unique weapons, and participate in a new TCG event, further diversifying the gameplay experience.

Conclusion

Genshin Impact Update 4.3 is a testament to miHoYo’s commitment to keeping the game fresh, exciting, and engaging for its vast global community. With new characters, weapons, and events, Update 4.3 promises to be a thrilling experience for both seasoned players and newcomers alike. The anticipation for December 20, 2023, is palpable, as players around the world prepare to immerse themselves in the new adventures that await in Teyvat.
