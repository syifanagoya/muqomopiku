Eva Longoria Dating, Steps Out With New Boyfriend Jose Antonio Baston

Eva Longoria's love life is revving up again! The actress, 38, stepped out for the first time with new love Jose "Pepe" Antonio Baston at the Museo Jumex Opening dinner in Mexico City on Friday, Nov. 15 with the handsome, 45-year-old businessman. A source confirms to Us Weekly, "They have been seeing each other for a few weeks. He's super sweet to her and a complete gentleman."

The Desperate Housewives star beamed for the camera in a classy, form-fitting, sleeved, black-and-white body-con dress. She completed her sophisticated ensemble with pointed stiletto pumps and a black, satin clutch with gold hardware. 

Baston, meanwhile, is a worthy match for the Devious Maids producer: The business executive is president of Televisa, Latin America's largest media company, and the world's largest Spanish-speaking media conglomerate, according to his official biography page for the company.

Longoria and her new love were first spotted kissing and holding hands in NYC in late October. The source adds to Us, "They have been spending a lot of time together and like each other a lot."
