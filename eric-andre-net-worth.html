Eric Andre Full Overview and Wiki

The Early Years and Education

Eric Samuel André, better known as Eric Andre, was born on April 4, 1983, in Boca Raton, Florida.

His background is a blend of diverse cultures. His father is an Afro-Haitian immigrant who works as a psychiatrist, while his mother hails from New York’s Upper West Side and is of Ashkenazi Jewish descent.

His upbringing was influenced by this cultural mix, which later became a source of inspiration for his comedy.

He received his education at the Dreyfoos School of the Arts and went on to earn a Bachelor of Music degree from Berklee College of Music in 2005, showcasing his creative talents from an early age.

The Rise to Fame

His journey to fame began in 2003 when he embarked on his entertainment career. However, it was in 2012 that he gained widespread recognition as the creator, host, and co-writer of The Eric Andre Show.
