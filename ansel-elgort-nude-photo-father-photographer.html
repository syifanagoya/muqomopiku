Ansel Elgort Shared a Fully Nude Photo of Himself (Taken by His Father)

Say what you will about Ansel Elgort (remember when he flooded his grid with brooding shirtless selfies?), but boy knows how to get people talking.

On Tuesday, the Goldfinch star shared a tasteful photo of himself in the shower, dripping wet and fully nude (save for a gold chain around his neck), his hands carefully placed, allowing the photo to circumvent Instagram’s strict nudity restrictions.

The caption reads “OnlyFans LINK IN BIO,” referring to the exclusive social media platform that is often used to share explicit imagery. Of course, that’s not where the link actually leads … Elgort’s bio opens a Go Fund Me page for Brooklyn for Life, a fundraiser for Brooklyn hospital workers as well as the local restaurants that have been affected by the coronavirus pandemic.

It’s great that Elgort is using his influence for good, but back to the photo for a moment. Said shower shot was taken by Arthur Elgort, who is an established photographer, and has worked with everyone from Madonna to well, his son. It's art. It's familial. And now perhaps a challenge to other Hollywood friends?
