Bio and Early Life

Harry John has identical twins brother, Luke Treadaway, who is also an actor and an older brother, Sam Treadaway, a multimedia artist. He studied at Queen Elizabeth's Community College along with his twin brother Luke where they won the Devon Senior Cup with the Rugby Union team.
