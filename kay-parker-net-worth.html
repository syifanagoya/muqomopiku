What is Kay Parker's Net Worth?

Kay Parker is an English metaphysical counselor and former adult film actress who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Kay Parker was born in Birmingham, England in August 1944. She was introduced the pornography industry by actor John Leslie. From 1977 to 1998 she appeared in more than 60 adult films including the Taboo series. As a child actress Parker appeared in an episode of the TV series It's a Man's World in 1962.

She also worked as public relations representative for Caballero Home Video. Parker authored her autobiography Taboo: Sacred, Don't Touch in 2001. She has a YouTube channel where she mentors those looking for spiritual and personal growth. She was inducted in the AVN and XRCO Halls of Fame and in 2017 she was named to the Top 12 Spirited Woman Book Pick List. In 2017 she appeared in the documentary film A Taboo Identity.
