Teju Babyface Full Overview and Wiki

A Journey Through Comedy and Television

Teju Babyface, born Gbadewonuola Olateju Oyelakin, embarked on his entertainment journey in 1998, initially shining in Tade Ogidan’s movie Diamond Ring.

This marked the beginning of a promising acting career, further spotlighted by his role in Solitaire opposite Richard Mofe-Damijo and appearances in other TV series.

However, Teju’s passion for comedy led him down a path that would eventually crown him as The King of Talk in Nigeria.

The Rise of The Teju Babyface Show

In 2010, Teju introduced a new genre of talk to the Nigerian TV space with The Teju Babyface Show.

This platform not only showcased his wit and interviewing prowess but also played host to celebrities and personalities, further cementing his place in the entertainment industry.

The show, running for over a decade, has been a critical factor in his net worth’s steady climb, highlighting the success that can come from innovative television production.

Ambassadorial Roles and Literary Contributions

In 2017, his influence extended beyond entertainment as he was appointed a United Nations Sustainable Development Goals Ambassador for Nigeria.
