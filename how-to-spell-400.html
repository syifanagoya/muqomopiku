How do you spell 400 dollars on a check?

$ (Amount in Numeric Form): Put 400.00 in the box right after the $ sign on the same line. Make sure to include the decimal part 00. DOLLARS (Amount in Words): Write Four hundred and 00/100 on the next field as far to the left on that line as possible. Use sentence case.
