Due To Overwhelming Demand Imagine Dragons Adds Five New Shows To Nearly Sold Out Summer Tour

LOS ANGELES, March 28, 2013 /PRNewswire/ — Imagine Dragons, one of the biggest break-out rock bands of 2012, adds five new shows to their highly anticipated summer tour for a total of 19 dates.  They now stop at Bank of America Pavilion in Boston, Massachusetts on July 24th, Nikon at Jones Beach Theater in Wantagh, New York on July 25th, Woods Amphitheater at Fontanel in Nashville, Tennessee on July 27th, Echo Beach in Toronto, Ontario on July 29th, and Jacobs Pavilion at Nautica in Cleveland, Ohio on July 30th.  The summer tour begins on May 7th in Charlotte, North Carolina at the Time Warner Cable Uptown Amphitheatre.  Tickets for the newly announced shows go on sale April 5th on LiveNation.com.  Tickets for the previously announced shows are available now.

Citi® cardmembers will have access to presale tickets beginning Tuesday, April 2nd at 10am local time through Citi’s Private Pass® Program. For complete presale details visit www.citiprivatepass.com.  Beginning today fans can RSVP to the Facebook presale by clicking here or visiting  http://on.fb.me/XDgEng to RSVP for early access to presale tickets available on Thursday, April 4th.  Live Nation mobile app users will have access to presale tickets on Thursday, April 4th.  Mobile users can text “LNAPP” to 404040 to download the Live Nation mobile app. (Available for iOS and Android.) 

Imagine Dragons also confirm Paper Route, Envy Corps, X Ambassadors, Nico Vega, Moth and the Flame and Terraplane Sun will open in select cities in May and June. Support for the July shows will be announced soon.       

This evening on the Tonight Show with Jay Leno
, Imagine Dragons will perform their No. 1  single, “Radioactive,” from their gold-certified debut album, Night Visions (KIDinaKORNER/Interscope Records).  Imagine Dragons will also premiere a recorded live performance of their song “Radioactive” on Friday, April 5th at 9 AM PT/12 Noon ET on LiveNation.com. The video clip was captured on February 9
th during the band’s first headlining tour in their hometown of Las Vegas at The Joint at Hard Rock Hotel & Casino. The crowd of “we-know-every-word” erupted when the band performed their hit single. A sneak peek of the video can be seen by clicking here.

The summer tour comes on the heels of their sold out club tour with the band performing in larger theaters and boutique amphitheaters.  Imagine Dragons have already sold out the historic Red Rocks Amphitheater in Denver, two nights at the world-famous Palladium in Los Angeles, as well as shows in San Diego, Salt Lake City and Boise.

Imagine Dragons are:  Dan Reynolds (vocals), Wayne Sermon (guitar), Ben McKee (bass), and Daniel Platzman (drums). The band is touring in support of Night Visions (KIDinaKORNER/Interscope Records) which debuted at No. 2 on the Billboard Album chart selling more than 83,000 copies — the best first-week sales for a full-length album by a new rock band since May 2006. Night Visions also climbed to No. 1 on Billboard‘s Rock and Alternative Albums chart. The first single “It’s Time” spent 41 weeks on the Modern Rock chart, 44 weeks on the Triple A chart and reached the Top 10 at Top 40 and Hot AC charts, selling more than 2 million copies.  Their second single “Radioactive” has sold over 1 million copies and is currently No. 1 on the Modern Rock chart for seven weeks running.
