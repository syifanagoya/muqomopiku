Life-Saving Treatment Makes Eric A Changed Man

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers suggest that Eric Forrester’s (John McCook) life-saving treatment could make him a changed man. The patriarch returned home on the Thursday, January 18 episode.

He was brought back to life. Questions still lie as to how he’ll react to this medical experiment. Read on to learn more.

B&B Spoilers: Will Eric’s Personality Change?

According to The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers, Eric could become a different person. This question was brought up on the SoapCentral.com Message Boards. The user lovely rita that he could have a different perspective on life. After all, he almost crossed over to the other side.

The B&B fan asked: “Do you think going over to the other side, which he described as peaceful, beautiful, no pain in his body anymore, hearing music, seeing Stephanie, will that make Eric a different man with a different outlook on life or different personality?”

He might not become the success-driven man that he was during the fashion showdown. Instead, Eric might want to slow down and enjoy time with his family. The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers suggest that he could have a new lease on life. Eric could take a break from his work.

One user responded: “No, he will be a different man for a little while, but he will go back to being the same old Eric once he is home for a while and is feeling better. Leopards do not change their spots. Just my opinion.”

Another fan wrote: “Exactly. And how about Thomas and Steffy? They should’ve been there. I am so sick of lousy writing.”

The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers: Eric Returns Home

According to The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers, Eric returned home to his family. He was surrounded by Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye), Brooke Logan (Katherine Kelly Lang), and his wife Donna Logan (Jennifer Gareis). Carter Walton (Lawrence Saint-Victor) joined them as well.

The reaction to his return has been mixed. Some B&B fans found it too cheesy and saccharine. Other fans wanted to see the rest of the Forresters with Eric. Others think it’s fascinating that Bell and his writers are focusing on Eric’s spiritual journey. It could suggest that he might live life differently.

What are your thoughts on Eric returning home? Do you think he’ll become a changed man? Or, do you think it’ll be the same thing? Sound off below in the comment section.
