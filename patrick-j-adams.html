Patrick J. Adams

Adams in 2014, photo by Prphotos.com

Birth Name: Patrick Johannes Adams

Place of Birth: Toronto, Ontario, Canada

Date of Birth: August 27, 1981

Ethnicity: German, English, possibly other

Patrick J. Adams is a Canadian actor. He is known for his role in the television series Suits.

He is the son of Rowan Marsh and journalist Claude Adams. His father was born in 1948 in West Germany, and moved to Sherbrooke, Québec, Canada, in 1952. Claude is a lapsed Catholic.

Patrick is married to American actress Troian Bellisario, with whom he has two children.

Patrick’s paternal grandparents are named Johannes/Hans Adams and Inge.

Patrick’s maternal grandfather was named Victor J. Marsh (the son of Sylvia Annie). Victor was born in England.

Patrick’s maternal grandmother was named Patrcia R. Greer.
