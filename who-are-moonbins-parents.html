Who are Moonbins Parents?

Who are Moonbin’s Parents? – Moon Bin, a talented South Korean singer, actor, dancer, and model, was born on January 26, 1998.

He was signed under the renowned label Fantagio and was a member of the popular South Korean boy group Astro, where he also belonged to the sub-unit Moonbin & Sanha.

Moon B in started his career as a child model and ulzzang in 2004, with the encouragement of his mother. He made his first known appearance in 2006, appearing in TVXQ’s music video for their song “Balloons,” where he portrayed a mini U-Know Yunho. Later in 2009, he had his first acting role in the Korean drama Boys over Flowers, where he played the younger version of Kim Bum’s character.

Moon Bin had been training with Fantagio since he was in fifth grade and became a full-fledged trainee during his middle school years. He was introduced as the second trainee of the company’s boy group project iTeen with the Fantagio iTeen Photo Test Cut.

In 2016, Moon Bin debuted with Astro as part of the six-member boy group, and they released their debut extended play, Spring Up. The EP contained five songs, including the lead single “Hide & Seek.”

Moon Bin went on to have a successful career in both acting and music. He appeared in various Korean dramas, such as Moments of Eighteen, and hosted Korean music show, Show Champion. He also starred in web-dramas like The Mermaid Prince, where he played the lead role.

In addition, Moon Bin joined the eco-friendly cooking program Food Avengers and was announced as one of the fashion models for the brand “Nerdy Cafe.” He also received recognition for his talents when he was selected as Neikidnis’ first muse in September 2021.

On December 30, 2022, Fantagio announced that Moon Bin had renewed his contract with the agency. His passing on April 19, 2023, was a great loss to the K-pop industry, and his fans around the world mourned his passing.

Who are Moonbin’s Parents?

Details about Moonbin’s parents are not known, however, considering how he made it in terms of music at a young age, his parents were quite supportive.
