Best dressed celebrities in December 2023: Natalie Portman, Beyonc, Cher, more

As the late Vivienne Westwood once said, "Fashion is life-enhancing". She had a point. 

After a gruesome set of Halloween looks from Hollywood's best dressed, it was out with the horror and in with the haute couture as November opened its doors to a fleet of fashionable stars.

Latex, leather, vinyl and velour galore, we saw a cocktail of head-turning looks from the style set this season as film premieres and A-lister parties cropped up in the calendar.  And that's just November. There are plenty of festive styles to look forward to as we head into December.

From Natalie Portman to Beyoncé, keep scrolling to discover which stars served in the style stakes this December so far...

Best dressed celebrities in November 2023

Naomi Campbell

Naomi Campbell embodied regal brilliance at "The Absence Of Eden" screening during the Red Sea International Film Festival on 2 December. 

Wearing an embellished crimson gown, the British supermodel's Regencycore ensemble also boasted larger-than-life puffed sleeves and a rippling train. 

Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish leaned into her signature eclectic streetwear style at the Variety's Hitmakers presented by Sony Audio on 2 December.

Cher

The 'Believe' singer looked incredible when she stepped on stage at the Royal Albert Hall this week for the Royal Variety Performance. 

The music icon was seen in a pair of studded black leather leggings, a matching leather corset and killer heeled boots. Her classic black long locks were a knockout finishing touch.

Beyoncé

Queen Bey looked incredible when she stepped out for the UK premiere of her much-anticipated concert film Renaissance. The 'Run the World' singer wore a black slinky dress with an incredible silver shield-like detail with diamantés and blue gems. 

Adding to the drama was the singer's platinum blonde hair and platform razor-sharp pointed-toe heels.

Natalie Portman

The Black Swan actress looked gorgeous in an understated peacoat mini dress with a belt and high neck at the special screening of her new movie, May December. Natalie added leg-lengthening black boots and wore her brunette hair in a loose natural style.

Her classic wing liner and natural glossy lip were in keeping with the effortless aesthetic.

Nicole Scherzinger

The Masked Singer panellist channelled none other than music icon Cher this week. The former Pussycat Doll looked incredible in a white halterneck crop top and co-ordinating slinky skirt in a white shimmering fabric.

The inches of dark hair imitating the 'Believe' singer was the real showstopper.

Taylor Swift

The 'Cruel Summer' singer looked stunning when she arrived at the London premiere of Beyonce's Renaissance film. The hitmaker was seen in a stunning silver floor-length gown with a deep thigh-split and a corsage detail.

Of course, it wouldn't be a Taylor look if it wasn't complete with her trademark curls and statement red lip. 

Blake Lively

Blake Lively too pulled out all the stops at the London premiere of Beyonce's Renaissance movie. The Gossip Girl star was seen in a black mini skirt and cardigan co-ord with black sheer stockings underneath.

The longline coat she wore overtop added a touch of glamour and her pointed-toe Louboutin heels were so Serena van der Woodsen.

Julia Roberts

Julia Roberts stepped out in London on 29 November for a special London screening of her new movie, “Leave the World Behind,” showcasing her impeccable style and flair for fashion.

The acclaimed actress was the epitome of chic in a bold pink Gucci ensemble, featuring eye-catching shorts paired with a perfectly matched blazer. 

Her glittering slipper shoes, dripping with crystal tassels, were worthy of their own runway.

Dua Lipa

Miss Dua Lipa is seriously making us reconsider our next hair appointment with her fiery copper locks. The disco pop muse, who recently released her new single 'Houdini' stormed New York City in platform Mary Janes and an oversized wool coat. 

Leigh-Anne Pinnock

Little Mix's Leigh-Anne Pinnock was amongst a fleet of stars who turned out to celebrate the launch of Smirnoff's WE DO US campaign. 

In a noughties-inspired denim coord, the mother-of-two captivated in her edgy ensemble. 

In collaboration with Tilting the Lens, the 26 Leake Street venue was transformed into a fully accessible and welcoming space, including a quiet area for a moment of calm, a dedicated viewing platform for wheelchair users, step-free access to the venue, BLS interpreters, an accessible bar and accessible toilets.

Rachel Zegler

The Hunger Games star Rachel Zegler wore a Dior Spring-Summer 2024 black tulle embroidered dress, black shoes, an ebony Lady D-Joy bag, and glittering jewelry to turn heads at Dior's Carousel of Dreams at Saks.  

Jennifer Lawrence

Dior muse Jennifer Lawrence was a monochrome maven at Dior's Carousel of Dreams at Saks Holiday window and light show unveiling in New York City. 

The Hollywood maven donned a Dior Spring-Summer 2024 black wool coat, white cotton shirt, and a black silk skirt. 

Arianna DeBose

Leading lady Ariana DeBose danced onto the blue carpet at the London premiere of Disney's Wish in a sweeping purple evening gown on 20 November. 

Paul Mescal & Andrew Scott

When Normal People's Connell Waldron crossed paths with Fleabag's Hot Preist...

Paul Mescal and Andrew Scott stepped out together at a private screening of their new movie All of Us Strangers in Los Angeles on 20 November. Paul, 27, looked suave in a navy tee and grey suit trousers, while Andrew, 47, opted for a bold striped shirt and wide-leg pants. 

Katherine Jenkins

Katherine Jenkins OBE looked sublime in a berry-red festive coat and chunky leather boots as she as she headed to LaplandUK with her family.

Cynthia Erivo

Cynthia Erivo was a monochrome maven in a billowing black and white gown to attend the 'Divas Simply Singing' concert in Los Angeles. 

David Tennant

David Tennant donned a glittering velvet jacket to attend the 67th Evening Standard Theatre Awards at Claridge's Hotel. Looking like he had stepped out of the Wizarding World, the Harry Potter and Doctor Who star leaned into fantastical fabrics for his suave get-up. 

Margot Robbie

If you thought Barbie fever had passed in the summer, Margot Robbie proved on 18 November that her plastic fantastic doppelganger hasn't dissappeared just yet.

The Australian actress joined director Greta Gerwig for a screening and Q&A at Linwood Dunn Theater on Saturday, serving up a slumber party Barbie aesthetic in a sily co-ord adorned with feathered sleeves and hems.

Dua Lipa

Dua Lipa is officially in her copper era and we can't get enough. The Houdini hitmaker hit 2023 Variety Power Of Women event in a red satin corset dress, making her glossy auburn hair the talking point of her outfit.

Serena Williams

Megan Fox

Megan Fox, 37, looked stunning when she sat down with Bustle's Samantha Leach to discuss her new book, Pretty Boys are Poisonous, wearing a leather midi-skirt and sheer tank top ensemble in a warm brown hue.

She paired the outfit with black knee-high boots in a patent leather. The look came shortly after Megan's major hair rehaul where she chopped off her long raven locks and dyed them bright red. We are major fans.

Heidi Klum

America's Got Talent judge Heidi Klum, 50, looked like a total goddess on the red carpet of America's Got Talent: Fantasy League. The supermodel was seen in a metallic floor-length gown that cinched and the waist and flowed into a dramatic pleated skirt.

The one-shoulder neckline created an elegant silhouette and as we would expect, Heidi's hair and makeup looked uber glamorous. That 70s-inspired haircut with the sweeping fringe just never gets old.

Natalie Portman

Natalie Portman, 42, arrived Netflix's May December premiere in Los Angeles channelling her Black Swan moment from the 2010 movie she starred in. The actress wore a black mini dress with a unique skirt, reminiscent of a tutu, that looked like a wave that was covered in black gems and sequins.

The star of the show was a large feather detail on the corseted bodice that added an extra touch of drama.

Dua Lipa

'Dance The Night' singer Dua Lipa, 28, looked fabulous when she chose a dress to match her new, ultra-trendy cherry cola hair at the Variety Power of Women event. The singer opted for a figure-skimming satin style with a pretty bow detail on the bust.

Her new brightly-coloured hair was styled in loose waves and she carried a stylish Vivienne Westwood clutch in a croc texture. We also loved seeing her intricate fine-line tattoos adorning her arms.

Chrissy Teigen

Model Chrissy Teigen  looked breathtaking in a shimmering green dress that made her look like a real-life flower. The mother-of-four, 37, arrived at the Cult Gaia RE24 Runway Show alongside her Voice coach husband, John Legend, in a dress which was made from a high-shine plissé fabric that almost looked like gilded metal.  

Her gold accessories added to the ethereal mermaid-like feel of the look and her new bobbed hair was understated and elegant.

Her husband looked equally stylish. John wore a monochrome look with a more casual aesthetic.

Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish played into her instantly identifiable silhouette in a bespoke Gucci coord, oversized and heavily influenced by streetwear. The Happier Than Ever artist added to her ensemble as added a firey red root to her raven hair. 

Elliot Page

Gucci muse Elliot Page looked dapper in a sharp tailored suit at the 2023 LACMA Art+Film Gala, Presented By Gucci on 4 November.

Jennifer Lopez

A moment of silence is needed for Jennifer Lopez in this dress. 

Joining a fleet of stars at the LACMA Art+Film Gala, Presented By Gucci, JLo graced the red carpet in a sheer lace gown adorned with a satin Champagne-hued bralette that highlighted her breathtaking ruby choker. 

Kim Kardashian

Kim Kardashian served up big Barbie energy in a billowing hot pink gown and glamorous opera gloves at the LACMA Art+Film Gala, Presented By Gucci.

Paris Hilton

Paris Hilton was a modern Cinderella in a billowing powder blue gown to attend the 2023 LACMA Art+Film Gala, Presented By Gucci on 4 November. The star danced into the venue with a cascading ponytail and beguiling beauty glam that highlighted her delicate features.

Lupita Nyong'o

Lupita Nyong'o looked ethereal in a tulle maroon dress by Gucci, embodying a ballerina aesthetic to attend the 2023 LACMA Art+Film Gala.

Salma Hayek

Salma Hayek pulled out all the stops in a fitted metallic pencil skirt to attend the opening reception for her brother Sami Hayek's show: FREQUENCY at Christie's Beverly Hills.

Sofia Vergara

Sofia Vergara looked gorgeous in a scarlet dress with a cut-out on the neckline, long fluted sleeves and an asymmetrical hemline at Sami Hayek's show. The AGT star styled her honied locks in a perfectly sleek.

Kylie Jenner

Timothée Chalamet

Sharp tailoring reigned supreme for Wonka star Timothée Chalamet, who looked slick and sophisticated in a monochrome ensemble to support his girlfriend Kylie Jenner at the Wall Street Journal Magazine 2023 Innovator Awards.

Nicole Richie

90s muse Nicole Richie made a case for animal print in a vibrant tiger-print top and gothic maxi skirt to attend the third annual Día de Muertos Celebration at Hollywood Forever on 1 November. 

Slicking back her honey-blonde hair, adding a timeless red lip and layering with a fur-trimmed jacket, Nicole redefined Hollywood glamour with her eclectic mix-n-match ensemble. 

Ed Westwick & Amy Jackson

Ed Westwick sported a timeless combination of denim jeans and a black tee to join his girlfriend Amy Jackson at the Youth To The People X World Vegan Day event in London on 1 November. 

His muse Amy amped up the glamour in a corseted mini dress and vampy vinyl jacket, slipping into thigh-high leather boots and sealing her look with a plum-hued lipstick.

A$AP Rocky

If there was ever a colour more suited to autumn, it's orange. Rihanna's beau and father-of-two A$AP Rocky stepped out in Beverly Hills in an electric orange coat, cargo pants and leather brogues, making a case for genderless fashion in the current It-girl accessory - the Bottega Veneta drop earrings.
